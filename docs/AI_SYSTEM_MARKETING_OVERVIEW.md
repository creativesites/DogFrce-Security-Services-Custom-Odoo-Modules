## What DogForce AI Is

## Ten Features That Work Where You Work

### 1. Attendance Anomaly Detection
*On every payslip — one click before you process pay*

The AI scans a guard's attendance records for the pay period and flags genuine concerns: unusual AWOL patterns, a week of consecutive late arrivals, excessive unpaid hours that don't match approved leave. It gives you a bullet-point report with the specific metric and a recommended action — before the payslip is confirmed.

> *"3 AWOL occurrences this period — 2 more than the previous month. Reliability score has dropped 8 points. Recommend disciplinary review before pay confirmation."*

---

### 2. Guard Risk Profiling
*On every guard's profile — quarterly or on demand*

Based on 90 days of operational history — attendance, incidents, leave, document compliance, reliability score — the AI rates each guard LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / CRITICAL and writes a plain-English narrative explaining exactly why. No more gut-feel assessments. Objective, consistent, evidence-based.

> *"MEDIUM risk. Attendance rate of 84% is below the site average. Two AWOL incidents in 90 days, with a pattern of Mondays and Fridays. No disciplinary record. Recommend a supervisor conversation and attendance monitoring."*

---

### 3. Billing Auditor
*On every invoice — cross-check before you send to clients*

The AI compares every line on your invoice against actual attendance records for the same client and period. It flags overbilling, underbilling, rate discrepancies, and missing lines — with specific figures. Catch N$3,000 overbilling errors before your client does.

> *"WARNING: Gate 1 post billed for 440 hours. Attendance records show 423 worked hours. Potential overbilling of 17 hours × N$45/hr = N$765. Verify against overtime approvals."*

---

### 4. Roster Optimizer
*On every roster batch — before you confirm assignments*

When you have unassigned slots, the AI analyses your available guard pool — scores, grades, site familiarity, workload fairness — and recommends optimal assignments. It accounts for guards who are excluded from sites, those with expiring documents, and those who've already worked too many consecutive shifts.

---

### 5. Smart Shift Fill
*On every absent or AWOL attendance record — immediate replacement*

A guard calls in sick. The AI immediately scans your available pool for that date — excluding already-scheduled guards, site-excluded guards, and guards on approved leave — and returns your top 3 replacement recommendations ranked by grade match, reliability score, and workload fairness. Replacement decisions in seconds, not phone calls.

> *"#1: Guard T. Shikongo — Grade A, reliability 91/100, only 18 shifts this month. Site-familiar. No expiring documents."*

---

### 6. Incident Consequence Advisor
*On every behavioral incident — before the disciplinary letter*

When a guard is written up, the AI reviews the incident against the guard's 12-month history — previous incidents, types, deductions, recurrence patterns — and recommends an appropriate consequence on the progressive discipline scale: verbal warning, written warning, final warning, suspension, or dismissal. It includes specific language you can use in the disciplinary letter and lists mitigating and aggravating factors.

Consistent. Defensible. Compliant with Namibian labour law principles.

---

### 7. Leave Coverage Check
*On every leave request — before you approve*

Before you approve a guard's leave request, the AI calculates exactly what it means for your operations. It shows which of their assigned sites will be affected, how many other guards are already on leave at those sites during the same period, and the estimated remaining coverage. It flags CRITICAL GAPs before you create them — not after.

> *"Site Alpha will have 2 guards on leave simultaneously on 14–16 June, leaving only 1 guard for a 3-post site. Recommend staggered approval or replacement arrangement before confirming."*

---

### 8. Document Renewal Letter
*On every expiring guard document — professional notice in one click*

When a guard's ID, firearm certificate, or police clearance is expiring, the AI drafts a formal, professional renewal notice addressed to the guard — specifying the document, expiry date, submission deadline, required renewal materials, and the consequence of non-renewal. Ready to print or email.

No more copy-pasting old letters and forgetting to update the dates.

---

### 9. Guard Performance Review
*On every guard — quarterly review in 30 seconds*

The AI generates a complete formal quarterly performance review based on 90 days of operational data: attendance rate, punctuality, AWOL incidents, discipline record, document compliance, and reliability trend. Four dimensions rated individually, overall rating assigned (OUTSTANDING / SATISFACTORY / NEEDS IMPROVEMENT / UNSATISFACTORY), with narrative and development recommendations. Ready for the personnel file.

---

### 10. Payslip Plain-English Explanation
*On every payslip — transparency for your guards*

Guards often don't understand their payslips, leading to disputes and trust issues. The AI translates every earning and deduction line into plain, simple language — explaining what each item is, how it was calculated, and why it was deducted. Reduces payroll queries dramatically and builds trust with your workforce.

> *"You worked 22 days this month. Your basic pay is 22 × N$230/day = N$5,060. Your PAYE tax is N$380 — this is required by law based on your income bracket. Your SSC deduction is N$180 — this goes towards your social security fund..."*

## The Global AI Assistant

**Ask anything. Navigate anywhere. Act — with your approval.**

The DogForce AI Assistant is a floating chat panel available on every screen. It's not a FAQ bot. It has real-time access to your live operational data and can search, navigate, and act — all within a single conversation.

### What it can do

**Find information instantly**
> "How many guards were AWOL this week?"
> "Show me all invoices overdue by more than 30 days"
> "Who are the top 10 guards by reliability score?"
> "What's the attendance rate at Site Bravo this month?"

The assistant searches your live data and presents the results as an interactive table. Click any row to open that record.

**Navigate you to the right place**
> "Take me to John Nangolo's profile"
> "Show me the current payroll period"

One click on a navigation chip opens the exact record. No more hunting through menus.

**Propose and execute actions — with your approval**
> "Approve the leave request from Sarah Hamukoto"
> "Mark payslip #PS-2026-0042 as confirmed"

The assistant proposes the action with a full description of what it will do. You click **Confirm**. Only then does anything change. You are always in control.

**Give you a real-time operations picture**
> "What needs my attention right now?"

The assistant pulls all pending actions — leave requests to approve, incidents to review, unassigned roster slots, overdue invoices, expiring documents — and presents them in one clean summary with direct links to each item.
